About

Creating art with technology is a fascination of mine. Being a recent graduate from the Coding Dojo and Cal State University Fullerton I have learned about new technologies and the latest design techniques.

I started as a Civil Engineer major and later decided that it was not for me. I changed my major to Art and emphasized in Graphic Design. The Graphic Design major offered some web classes and that is when I knew that I wanted to learn more about coding. Outside of school I taught myself how to code because at CSUF they only taught the bare minimum of creating a web page.

I knew I wanted to continue my education in coding but did not want to take on another four years getting a CS degree. Upon searching on how to continue my education I found the Coding Dojo. Coding Dojo is a school that teaches three full stacks. I chose to take LAMP, MEAN, and ROR. I really enjoyed learning so much and am continuing to learn constantly.

During my free time when I am not coding I enjoy airbrushing 3D figurines and hanging out with my dogs.
